## Why SPC Is a Perfect Buyer for Tradex

SPC d.o.o. is a **growing Slovenian company** that already sells and rents industrial equipment (containers, heaters, fencing, construction machinery). Here's why Tradex fits their business perfectly:

### 1. They Already Sell Used Equipment
SPC sells **second-hand machines, containers, and equipment** alongside new products. An auction platform is a natural revenue channel for used equipment — higher prices through competitive bidding vs. fixed-price listings on their current shop.

### 2. Their Vision Explicitly Mentions Technology + Online Systems
From their website: *"Develop an advanced online quoting system to save customers time"* and *"Adopt advanced technologies to continually upgrade service quality."* Tradex IS that technology upgrade.

### 3. Geographic Expansion Ambition
SPC wants to expand from **Central Europe & Balkans to full European market**. Tradex is built for exactly this — 27-country EU compliance, 7 languages, cross-border VAT handling.

### 4. Right Size & Right Stage
With EUR 4.19M revenue and 9 employees, SPC is at the inflection point where a technology platform could 10x their reach without proportionally growing headcount. An auction platform turns them from a local equipment dealer into a European marketplace operator.

### 5. B2B Customer Base Already Exists
900+ customers in Central Europe & Balkans = a ready-made buyer pool for an auction platform. No cold-start problem.

## Presentation Strategy: What Matej Cares About

Matej Zupan is a **hands-on business owner** (not a VC or corporate executive). He thinks in terms of:

- **"Can I make money with this?"** — Show revenue model (transaction fees, higher selling prices via auction vs. fixed price)
- **"Does it work?"** — Live demo, not slides. He needs to SEE the platform working.
- **"Can my team run this?"** — Show the admin dashboard. 9 employees = it must be simple to operate.
- **"What about my market?"** — Show Slovenia, Croatia, Austria, Germany, Italy (his actual market). Not generic "EU" talk.
- **"How much will it cost me?"** — Be ready with numbers (but don't lead with price — lead with value)

### Language & Tone
- Speak practically, not academically
- Use concrete examples from HIS industry (containers, heaters, construction equipment)
- Reference HIS customers and markets (Slovenia, Balkans, DACH region)
- Avoid abstract tech jargon — translate everything to business outcomes

## Customized Presentation Flow (35-45 min)

### ACT 1: The Opportunity for SPC (5 min)

**Slide 1 — SPC's Growth Story**
- SPC grew from EUR 3.28M (2022) to EUR 4.19M (2024) — 28% growth in 2 years
- 900+ customers across Central Europe & Balkans
- Vision: expand to full European market with technology
- *"What if your next growth leap isn't more trucks and warehouses — it's a platform?"*

**Slide 2 — The Used Equipment Problem**
- SPC already sells used containers and equipment (fixed price or quote-based)
- Fixed prices leave money on the table — competitive bidding drives 15-30% higher prices
- Cross-border sales are manual (VAT calculations, compliance, language barriers)
- No visibility into demand from other EU countries

**Slide 3 — The Auction Platform Opportunity**
- EUR 150B in used industrial equipment traded annually in Europe
- Online auction market growing at 28.9% CAGR (2025-2029)
- Competitors (Troostwijk, Euro Auctions, IronPlanet) are large but NOT focused on containers & modular structures
- *"SPC can own the container & construction equipment auction niche for Central Europe & Balkans"*
- Show competitor logos: Troostwijk, Euro Auctions, IronPlanet, Ritchie Bros — then show the gap: none are Slovenian, none focus on containers, none serve the Balkans well

---

### ACT 2: Live Demo (20 min) — "Let Me Show You Your Platform"

> **Critical framing:** Throughout the demo, use SPC's name, products, and colors. This isn't a generic platform — this is *SPC's auction platform*.

**Step 1: The Buyer Experience (3 min)**
- Open buyer-web at `localhost:3000`
- Show hero section — point out it can be branded with SPC's logo and colors (#004d71)
- Show country selector with SI/HR/AT/DE/IT flags
- Scroll to category grid: "See — Containers, Climate Control, Construction Equipment — your product lines"
- Show CO2 counter: "Every container you resell avoids manufacturing a new one. This matters for EU corporate buyers."
- Switch language to German: "A buyer in Vienna sees this in German automatically"

**Step 2: SPC Team Lists Equipment (3 min)**
- Open seller portal — logged in as seller
- "Create Lot" -> fill in: "Pisarniski kontejner 6m — Custom Office Container"
- Category: Office Containers, Location: Ljubljana SI, Starting bid: EUR 4,500, Reserve: EUR 5,200
- Submit for review
- Say: "Your team creates the listing in 2 minutes. Photos, specs, starting price. Then it goes to review."

**Step 3: Matej Approves (3 min)**
- Open admin dashboard — logged in as admin
- "This is your control center, Matej. Everything on one screen."
- Show dashboard KPIs: active auctions, revenue, lots pending
- Navigate to Lot Approval -> show pending container listing -> click Approve
- Create Auction -> set times -> create
- Say: "You approve, you set the auction schedule, it goes live across all markets."

**Step 4: A Buyer from Zagreb Bids (4 min)**
- Back to buyer-web -> login as buyer
- Search "kontejner" or "container"
- Open the lot detail — show specs, location (Ljubljana), CO2 badge
- Place bid: EUR 4,600
- Say: "A construction company in Zagreb found your container. They bid EUR 4,600. The minimum increment keeps it fair."
- Explain: "If your starting price was EUR 4,500 fixed, you'd get EUR 4,500. With an auction, competitive demand can push this to EUR 5,500 or more."

**Step 5: Smart Auction Features (3 min)**
- **Anti-sniping:** "If someone bids in the last 2 minutes, the clock extends. No more losing money to last-second snipes. Every serious auction house uses this — now SPC has it too."
- **Proxy bidding:** "The buyer sets their max — say EUR 6,000 — and the system bids the minimum increment automatically. Like having an agent at a physical auction. This drives final prices up."
- **Tiered increments:** "EUR 1 increment for small items, EUR 100 for expensive machinery. Professional-grade."

**Step 6: Compliance (3 min)**
- Admin dashboard -> "GDPR Requests"
- Say: "When a customer in Germany asks to delete their data — which is their legal right under GDPR — you handle it with one click. Try doing that manually."
- Show Fraud Detection: "Shill bidding detection. If someone tries to artificially inflate prices, the system flags it."
- Show System Health: "All 13 services running. You see the health of everything."
- Say: "VAT is automatic. Selling a container from Ljubljana to a company in Zagreb? Reverse charge — 0% VAT, correctly calculated. To a German company? Same. 27 countries, all handled."

**Step 7: CO2 & Sustainability (2 min)**
- Seller portal -> CO2 Report
- Say: "Every container resold instead of scrapped avoids 8-12 tonnes of CO2. Your buyers can report this for their ESG compliance. And you, as a platform operator, can market this — 'Buy green, buy used, buy on SPC.'"
- Show PDF export: "Your sustainability report, downloadable."
