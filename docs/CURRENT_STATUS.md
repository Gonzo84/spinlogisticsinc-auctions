# SPC Auctions — EU B2B Online Auction Platform

## Your Complete Auction Business, Ready to Launch

SPC Auctions is a fully built, ready-to-deploy online auction platform designed specifically for the European B2B market. It handles everything — from the moment a seller lists industrial equipment to the moment payment is settled and the seller receives their payout.

Built for companies that auction machinery, industrial equipment, office inventory, vehicles, and commercial assets across Central and Southeast Europe.

---

## What SPC Auctions Does

### For Buyers

- **Find what you need** — Search thousands of lots by category, location, country, price range, or keyword. Results appear instantly with smart relevance ranking.
- **Bid with confidence** — Real-time bidding with live countdown timers. See every bid as it happens. Get notified immediately if someone outbids you.
- **Auto-bidding** — Set your maximum price and walk away. The system bids on your behalf, placing the minimum necessary to stay ahead — just like proxy bidding on major auction houses.
- **Fair auctions** — Anti-sniping protection automatically extends the auction if a bid is placed in the final minutes, giving every buyer a fair chance to respond.
- **Simple checkout** — Won an auction? Checkout is seamless. VAT is calculated automatically based on your country and business status. Pay and receive your invoice instantly.
- **Personal dashboard** — Track your active bids, won items, watchlist, and purchase history in one place.
- **8 languages** — Available in Slovenian, Croatian, German (Austria & Germany), Italian, Bosnian, Serbian, and Hungarian.

### For Sellers

- **List lots in minutes** — Upload photos (drag-and-drop, multiple images), describe your items, set a starting price and reserve, and submit for approval.
- **Track your sales** — Live dashboard showing active lots, total bids received, items sold, and revenue earned.
- **Get paid automatically** — When a buyer pays, your settlement is calculated instantly. Commission is deducted, and your payout amount is ready. Full transparency on every transaction.
- **Performance insights** — Monthly revenue charts, sell-through rates, and lot performance analytics help you optimize your auction strategy.
- **Sustainability reporting** — Every lot sold through SPC Auctions avoids manufacturing new equipment. See your CO2 savings with tree-equivalent and car-kilometer comparisons.

### For Brokers

- **Lead management** — Track leads from first contact through to lot creation. Status pipeline: New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted.
- **Lot intake** — Submit individual lots or upload batches via CSV for high-volume sourcing.
- **Dashboard** — Monitor your pipeline: leads generated, visits scheduled, lots submitted, conversion rates.

### For Platform Administrators

- **Full control** — Approve lots, create auctions, manage users, oversee payments, and monitor platform health from a single dashboard.
- **Auction management** — Create auctions, set schedules, feature premium lots on the homepage, award winners, or revoke awards if needed.
- **User oversight** — View user profiles, KYC verification status, manage roles (buyer, seller, broker, admin), and handle account issues.
- **Payment oversight** — Track all payments from pending through to settlement. Process refunds, settle payments, and view financial KPIs.
- **Fraud detection** — Monitor suspicious activity alerts and resolve fraud cases.
- **Compliance tools** — Handle GDPR data export/erasure requests, AML screening results, and DSA content reports.
- **Analytics** — Platform-wide revenue trends, bid volumes, user growth, and category popularity.

## The Auction Experience

### How an Auction Works on SPC Auctions

```
Seller lists a lot → Admin approves → Auction goes live → Buyers bid in real-time
    → Auction closes → Winner is awarded automatically → Buyer pays
        → VAT & commission calculated → Seller receives payout
```

**Every step is automated.** From the moment an auction closes, the winner is awarded, the payment record is created, VAT is calculated based on EU regulations, the seller's commission is deducted, and the settlement is ready — all without manual intervention.

### Real-Time Bidding

Bidding happens live. When someone places a bid, every viewer sees it update instantly on their screen — no page refresh needed. The countdown timer, current price, and bid count all update in real time.

**Anti-sniping protection** ensures fairness: if a bid arrives in the final minutes, the auction automatically extends, giving all participants time to respond. No more losing because someone bid in the last second.

**Auto-bidding** lets buyers set their maximum and let the system handle the rest. It places the minimum bid needed to stay in the lead, incrementing only when challenged — just like a professional auction house.

### Automated Payment Processing

| Step | What Happens | Who Benefits |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Auction closes | Winner determined, payment record created | Buyer, Seller |

- **Invoice generation** — every transaction produces a proper invoice with full VAT details

### Settlement & Payouts

- **Automated settlement** — once payment is confirmed, seller's payout is calculated instantly
- **Full audit trail** — every transaction, from bid to payout, is recorded and traceable

## Legal & Regulatory Compliance

SPC Auctions is built from the ground up for the European regulatory environment:

### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

- **Data export** — users can request a complete export of all their personal data (Article 20, right to data portability)
- **Data erasure** — users can request deletion of their data (Article 17, right to be forgotten), with cascading deletion across all platform services
- **Audit logging** — all data access and modifications are logged for accountability
- **Database isolation** — each service stores data independently, minimizing exposure in case of a breach

### PSD2 (Payment Services Directive)

- **Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)** ready — payment flow designed for 3D Secure and two-factor authentication
- **Secure payment processing** — payment gateway integration with Adyen (EU-certified PSP)

### DSA (Digital Services Act)

- **Content reporting** — users can report inappropriate content through a structured notice-and-action mechanism
- **Transparency reports** — platform generates DSA-compliant transparency reports on content moderation activity
- **Audit trail** — all moderation decisions are logged and traceable

### AML (Anti-Money Laundering Directive)

- **User screening** — AML screening interface for sanctions list checking
- **Fraud detection** — automated alerts for suspicious bidding patterns and transaction anomalies
- **KYC tracking** — Know Your Customer verification status tracked per user

### EU Consumer Protection

- **Fair auction rules** — anti-sniping protection ensures no buyer is disadvantaged by last-second bids
- **Clear pricing** — all fees (buyer premium, VAT, shipping) shown transparently before checkout
- **Bid history** — complete, auditable record of all bids placed

This gives your platform a genuine sustainability story — not just a marketing claim, but real, calculated environmental impact data.
