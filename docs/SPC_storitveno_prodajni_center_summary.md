## Business Activities & Product Offering

SPC d.o.o. is a comprehensive provider of standard and custom containers and modular structures, available for both **purchase and rental**.

### Containers

- **Office / Residential containers** – custom-built to order
- **Shipping containers** – new ISO, "first trip" (single-voyage), and used
- **Sanitary containers** – standalone units and in-container sanitary facilities
- **Modular container structures:**
  - Offices and conference rooms
  - Recreational units
  - Sanitary and shower units
  - Catering / food service units
  - Medical / first aid units
  - Storage spaces
  - Container houses

### Machinery & Equipment

| Category | Products |
|---|---|
| Heating | Electric, diesel, and gas heaters |
| Climate control | Dehumidifiers, humidifiers, air purifiers, air conditioning units, ozone generators, fans |
| Measurement | Moisture meters |
| Fencing & Construction | Metal fences, construction machinery and equipment, accessories |
| Used equipment | Resale of second-hand machines, containers and equipment |
| **Rental** | Equipment and machinery available for hire (published price list) |

The company operates its **own production facilities** for manufacturing office, sanitary and other modular containers. Contracted logistics partners provide road transport with on-site unloading.

## Summary

SPC d.o.o. is a financially stable, growing small Slovenian company with a top-tier credit rating (A+), specialising in the **sale, rental and production of containers** as well as the **sale and rental of construction equipment**. Founded and managed by director and majority owner Matej Zupan since 2012, the company exceeded **€4 million in revenue in 2024** with a net profit of over **€344,000**. With its own manufacturing facilities, a broad product range, and a proven track record of customer satisfaction, SPC positions itself as a reliable partner across Central Europe and the Balkans.
