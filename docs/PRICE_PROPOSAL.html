Phase 1 — Commercial Launch Requirements
20 March 2026

Phase 1 covers the items that must be completed before the platform can process real transactions and operate commercially in the EU. The second payment of EUR 7,500 is due upon delivery of these items.

1. Live Payment Processing (Adyen)
Connect the platform to Adyen to accept real payments — credit/debit cards, iDEAL, SEPA, Bancontact, and other European methods. Includes 3D Secure (PSD2), automatic refunds, and seller settlement timing. The payment flow is fully built; this connects it to the live processor.
2. Identity Verification (KYC)
Integrate a KYC provider (Onfido, Jumio, or IDnow) for automated identity verification — photo ID check, liveness detection, address verification, and risk scoring. Bidding blocked until verified. Required by EU financial regulations.
3. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Screening
Connect to an AML provider (ComplyAdvantage, Refinitiv, or LexisNexis) for sanctions list screening, PEP checks, adverse media monitoring, and suspicious transaction detection. Required by EU Anti-Money Laundering Directive (AMLD6).
4. Production Email Delivery
Switch from development mail capture to a production email service (AWS SES, SendGrid, or Mailgun). Branded templates, delivery tracking, bounce handling, and GDPR-compliant unsubscribe management.
5. GDPR Compliance Completion
Complete the automated data handling pipeline — cross-service data export (Article 20), automated data deletion (Article 17), 30-day deadline enforcement, privacy policy integration, and full audit trail for regulatory inspection.
6. Internal Security Hardening
Add authenticated service-to-service communication (mTLS or JWT). Every internal request becomes verified and auditable. Meets security requirements for financial service platforms and enterprise auditors.
7. Automated Deployment Pipeline Activation
Activate the production deployment stage — the CI/CD pipeline (build, test, scan, package) is already complete. This connects it to production infrastructure with one-click deploys and automatic rollback capability.

What Phase 1 Delivers

Upon completion of Phase 1, the platform is ready to process real money, verify real users, comply with EU regulations, and deploy to production automatically. This is the commercial launch point.
