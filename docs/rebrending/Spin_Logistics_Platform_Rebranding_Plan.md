# Spin Logistics Inc. — Platform Rebranding Plan

*Prepared March 21, 2026 | Based on live brand research across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Linktree, and carrier databases*

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document outlines the complete rebranding of the Tradex auction platform from its current identity ("SPC Aukcije" — an EU B2B industrial equipment auction platform) to **Spin Logistics Inc.** — a Chicago-based interstate freight carrier. The rebrand covers visual identity, color system, typography, tone of voice, content strategy, and all technical implementation touchpoints across the platform's 4 frontends, 13 microservices, and shared design token system.

## 2. BRAND RESEARCH FINDINGS

### 2a. Logo

The Spin Logistics logo was observed consistently across Instagram (profile picture, post watermarks), Facebook (profile picture), and Linktree.

**Description:**
- **Wordmark**: "SPIN" rendered in a custom/stylized **bold sans-serif** typeface, all caps
- The letter forms are clean and modern with slightly condensed proportions
- A **swoosh/speed line** runs beneath the wordmark, curving from left to right — evoking motion, speed, and forward momentum
- A small **dot/period** follows the wordmark (visible in post watermarks as "SPIN.")
- **Profile picture variant**: The SPIN wordmark is enclosed in a **green circle border** (ring/outline) on a white background — used on both Instagram and Facebook

**Logo Usage Observed:**
- **On dark backgrounds** (posts): White SPIN logo, bottom-left corner, small size — used as a watermark/brand stamp
- **On profile pictures**: White background, green circular border, SPIN centered
- **On Facebook cover**: Logo appears within the cover composition alongside "LOGISTICS INC" text

**Recommended digital logo variants to produce:**
1. Primary: SPIN wordmark + swoosh (white on dark)
2. Reversed: SPIN wordmark + swoosh (dark on white)
3. Icon: SPIN in green circle (for favicons, avatars, app icons)
4. Full: "SPIN LOGISTICS INC." horizontal lockup
5. Stacked: "SPIN" over "LOGISTICS INC." (for narrow contexts)

### 2c. Typography

**Observed font patterns from social media posts:**

| Context | Style | Closest Match |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| **Headlines** (HIRING, TRUCK DRIVERS!) | Bold, condensed, uppercase, high-impact | **Oswald** or **Barlow Condensed** (Google Fonts) — alternatively **Impact** or **Anton** |
| **Subheadings** (LEASE TO OWN PROGRAM) | Bold, uppercase, slightly lighter weight | **Montserrat Bold** or **Raleway Bold** |
| **Body text** (bullet points, descriptions) | Clean, readable, regular weight sans-serif | **Open Sans** or **Roboto** |
| **Logo wordmark** | Custom stylized bold sans-serif | Custom font / closest match: **Orbitron** or **Rajdhani** |

**Recommended Font Stack for Platform:**

```
Headings: 'Barlow Condensed', 'Open Sans', system-ui, sans-serif
Body: 'Open Sans', system-ui, sans-serif
Mono: 'JetBrains Mono', ui-monospace, 'Cascadia Code', monospace
```

**Rationale:** Barlow Condensed captures the bold, industrial, condensed headline style seen across Spin's Instagram graphics while remaining highly legible on screen. Open Sans (already in the current platform) works well as the body font. This combination bridges the gap between trucking-industry boldness and digital platform readability.

**Alternative heading font if a more polished look is desired:** `Montserrat` — slightly more refined, still bold and modern.

### 2d. Visual Tone & Photography Style

**Observed across 84 Instagram posts and Facebook content:**

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Photography** | Real trucks on highways, open roads, sunsets, industrial settings. Predominantly Volvo and Freightliner Cascadia rigs. Action shots with motion blur. |
| **Graphics** | Bold typographic compositions on dark or green gradient backgrounds. Heavy use of uppercase text. Geometric accent lines (angular/diagonal). Green checkmarks for lists. |
| **Mood** | Energetic, bold, forward-moving. "Get on the road" energy. Aspirational but grounded. |
| **Content mix** | ~40% recruiting posts, ~25% industry memes/relatable humor, ~20% informational (cargo types, tire safety, traffic), ~15% company culture/photos |
| **Composition** | Logo watermark bottom-left on all branded graphics. Clean information hierarchy with clear headings. |

**Design motifs observed:**
- Diagonal/angular geometric lines (speed, motion)
- Gradient overlays (dark-to-green, dark-to-amber)
- Circular framing elements (green circle around logo, circular photo crops)
- Green banner bars for CTAs ("LEASE TO OWN PROGRAM AVAILABLE")

### 2e. Brand Voice & Messaging

**Taglines observed:**
- "Your trusted logistics partner" (Instagram bio)
- "Highest level of transportation services as well as a safe & timely delivery!" (Linktree)
- "Drive with the best team ever!" (Facebook cover)
- "Taking care of our employees and team work is the key for success" (Facebook intro)
- "#spinningsuccessdaily" (branded hashtag)

**Messaging tone:**
- **Direct and action-oriented** — "Join us", "Take the wheel", "Contact us now"
- **Benefits-first** — Leads with what the driver/partner gets (fuel cards, 88% gross, lease-to-own)
- **Community/family-oriented** — Emphasizes teamwork, employee care, being part of something
- **Professional but approachable** — Not corporate-stiff, not overly casual. Sits in the middle.
- **Confident** — "Top-notch", "peak condition", "highest level"

**Hashtag strategy:** `#SpinLogistics #CustomerSatisfaction #LogisticsExcellence` + industry-standard logistics hashtags

#### File: `frontend/shared/design-tokens/tailwind.preset.ts`

```typescript
  50:  '#E8F5E9',
  100: '#C8E6C9',
  200: '#A5D6A7',
  300: '#81C784',
  400: '#66BB6A',

  800: '#2E7D32',
  900: '#1B5E20',
  950: '#0D3B12',
  DEFAULT: '#388E3C',
},
```

Update heading font:

```typescript
fontFamily: {
  heading: ['Barlow Condensed', 'Open Sans', 'system-ui', 'sans-serif'],
  body: ['Open Sans', 'system-ui', 'sans-serif'],
  mono: ['JetBrains Mono', 'ui-monospace', 'Cascadia Code', 'Fira Code', 'monospace'],
},
```

Update PrimeVue semantic primary tokens to use the new green scale (same hex values as above mapped to the `semantic.primary` object).

## 6. BRAND GUIDELINES SUMMARY

### Do's

- Use the **SPIN** wordmark with swoosh on all branded materials
- Maintain the **dark background + green accent** pattern for high-impact sections (hero banners, CTAs)
- Use **uppercase bold headings** — this is core to the Spin visual identity
- Keep messaging **direct, benefits-first, and action-oriented**
- Use the branded hashtag **#SpinningSuccessDaily** on all social content
- Maintain the green circle logo variant for small icons/favicons
- Use real photography of trucks, roads, and logistics operations

### Don'ts

- Don't use the old blue (`#0077A3`) color scheme — it conflicts with the green identity
- Don't use lowercase-only headings — the brand voice is bold and confident
- Don't use overly formal corporate language — Spin's tone is professional but approachable
- Don't stretch or modify the logo proportions
- Don't place the logo on busy/cluttered backgrounds without a dark overlay
- Don't use colors outside the defined palette without design team approval
